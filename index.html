Core Features

🎯 Market Scanning

Real-time monitoring of Axie Marketplace floor prices with advanced filtering by class, breed count, level, and custom part combinations.

📊 Yield Analysis

Probabilistic modeling of release yields using Monte Carlo simulations. Accounts for rarity, parts, and breeding count to estimate expected value.

💰 Price Oracle

Multi-source price feeds for ETH, RON, and Memento tokens via Katana DEX. Maintains accurate cost-basis for arbitrage calculations.

⚡ Risk Management

Value-at-Risk (VaR) metrics, position sizing based on probability thresholds. Configurable profit gates to control execution.

🔐 Ronin Integration

Native Ronin signing and contract interaction. Supports both dry-run testing and live execution with proper key management.

📈 Trade Reporting

Detailed trade logs with probability metrics, expected PnL, and execution history for performance analysis and optimization.

About ArbiBot

ArbiBot is an open-source statistical arbitrage bot designed for the Axie Infinity ecosystem. It identifies mispricings between marketplace axies and their implied value through release mechanics, enabling profitable trading strategies grounded in quantitative analysis.

The bot combines real-time market data, probabilistic yield modeling, and risk-adjusted execution to discover and capitalize on temporary inefficiencies in the marketplace—while maintaining strict risk controls and transparency.
