Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. Introduction

ArbiBot ("we," "us," "our," or "the Software") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Software and visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use ArbiBot, you may voluntarily provide information such as:

  • Configuration settings and parameters you input into the Software
  • API keys and credentials (which you manage locally and are not transmitted to our servers)
  • Contact information if you reach out to us via email

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (for analytics only)
  • Device information and usage patterns

2.3 Information NOT Collected by ArbiBot

The Software does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Private keys or wallet credentials (users manage these locally)
  • Personal trading data or transaction history (except as logged locally by the user)
  • Wallet addresses or blockchain account information to remote servers
  • Personally identifiable information without explicit consent

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve and optimize the Software and website
  • Analyze usage patterns and user behavior
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Most data processed by ArbiBot is stored locally on your machine
  • Configuration files and logs are not automatically uploaded
  • API requests made by the Software go directly to third-party services (Axie Marketplace, Katana, Ronin)
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect data transmitted via our website
  • No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

ArbiBot integrates with third-party services:

  • Axie Infinity Marketplace API: Subject to Sky Mavis privacy policies
  • Katana DEX: Subject to Katana's data policies
  • Ronin Blockchain: Public blockchain, transactions are immutable and publicly visible

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Review their privacy policies independently.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies for:

  • Session management and user preferences
  • Analytics (Google Analytics or similar services)
  • Security and fraud prevention

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may disclose information if:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights, privacy, or safety
  • Necessary to prevent fraud or security threats
  • You have given explicit consent

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You can request a copy of personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal limitations)
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of marketing communications and analytics tracking

9. Children's Privacy

ArbiBot is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. Logs and analytics data are typically retained for 90 days.

11. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be transferred across international borders. By using ArbiBot, you consent to such transfers.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date and posting the revised policy. Your continued use of the Software constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Access and delete personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

14. EU Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU, you have rights including:

  • Right to access, rectification, and erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
